Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

An advanced app that helps users evaluate the quality of images. Aesthetic intelligence assess the aesthetic appeal, providing instant feedback on how good the content looks based on specific criteria.

The purpose of Aesthetic Intelligence is to assist content creators, photographers, and designers in improving their visual media. By providing an AI-driven evaluation, users can understand what makes their images and videos more appealing and make informed decisions about editing or selecting their content.

Aesthetic Intelligence uses a scoring scale from 1 to 10. A score of 1 indicates poor quality, while a score of 10 represents exceptional quality. This scale may help you easily understand how your media ranks in terms of visual appeal.

The score is evaluated based on a combination of different aesthetic qualities. The AI analyzes the uploaded media for elements such as how well the colors work together, the composition of the image, the clarity of the subject, and the overall contrast. Each of these aspects contributes to the final score.

The score assigned to an image is based on several key elements:

  • Composition: How the visual elements are arranged.
  • Color Balance: The harmony and contrast of colors used in the media. of a photo.
  • Contrast: The difference between the light and dark areas in the media.
  • Subject Matter: An interesting subject can significantly influence the overall score.

You can upload various types of media, including both images and videos. Aesthetic Intelligence supports image file formats such as JPEG, PNG, and GIF.

The app enforces an upload limit per session to maintain optimal performance. This ensures that the server can handle the requests effectively without delays.

Scoring is provided almost instantly. The app is designed to quickly analyze the uploaded media using AI technology. However, the exact time may vary slightly depending on the file size and the number of uploads. Generally, you can expect rapid feedback to help you improve your content.

Your privacy and security are our top priorities. Uploaded files are processed in real-time and are not stored permanently on our servers. This means your content is not kept long-term, ensuring confidentiality. Furthermore, all interactions with the app are protected by secure technology.
